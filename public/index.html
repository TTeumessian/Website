Security in The Age of AGI

AI and autonomous agents are not the future—they are the present. Autonomous AI systems are rapidly transforming healthcare, education, media, governance, and nearly every other domain. Their influence is growing at an exponential rate, soon permeating every corner of the economy and world. But this transformation comes at a cost: an expanding, highly complex attack surface whose security is lagging dangerously far behind the pace of innovation. Misaligned military agents could compromise national defense. Agentic trading firms could manipulate and destabilize public markets. Disinformation could reach an unprecedented scale, reshaping public perception and political landscapes. The vast risks are largely unexplored and poorly understood. Like all technology, agents provide leverage—leverage that will drive unparalleled economic and technological progress and prosperity. But if they are compromised, that leverage can and will be turned against us by threat actors to catastrophic outcomes. In this emerging era, corporations and nations face a choice: secure these systems proactively or become victims of their own creation.
